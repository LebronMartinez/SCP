WHAT IS SCP

Welcome to the SCP Foundation, where the borders of reality are pushed and the mysterious becomes ordinary. Here, we dig into the unknown, documenting and containing occurrences that defy natural principles. Our objective is to discover and manage the unknown, assuring humanity's safety from the numerous anomalies that exist outside conventional comprehension. From curious objects to remarkable creatures, our comprehensive database describes these SCPs in depth, including their attributes, containment techniques, and current research. Whether you're an inquisitive reader or an aspiring writer, the SCP Foundation encourages you to delve into the darkest regions of speculative fiction and join our community in finding the unknown.
